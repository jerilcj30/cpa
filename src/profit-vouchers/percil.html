About Us

Each Persil® ProClean® product has been developed to make your laundry routine easier than ever. Persil products must meet your laundry needs, but also the high performance standards set for all products carrying the Persil® ProClean® name. That’s why Persil detergent has been recognized on numerous occasions: Performance and fabric care are the focus of Persil’s product research and development, always aiming for the best results for our customers. Backed by the powerful team at Henkel, we conduct extensive technical tests to ensure that we are offering quality that you can trust.

We use a special stain machine to dirty garments with common substances such as cocoa, make-up, lipstick, red wine or engine oil. The almost identical stains provide the ideal conditions for comparative wash tests, which are carried out using hundreds of washing machines made by different manufacturers. This product testing allows us to achieve our goal: to be a brand and technology leader, making your life easier, better and more beautiful. As a Henkel brand, innovation and advancement are key pillars of the Persil® ProClean® product strategy. Persil® ProClean® will continue to adapt as society transforms, needs change, and product performance expectations grow.

Tradition: Our Deep Roots in Laundry Care As early as 1907, the chemists at Henkel succeeded in revolutionizing laundry with Persil detergent. As a proud Henkel brand, Persil has continued to develop laundry detergents with care ever since. Persil has grown into international markets under brand names like Le Chat, Wipp, Dixan, Nadhif and Fab. You’ve heard it before: Persil® ProClean® Detergent provides a revolutionary clean. So why not give Persil Discs a try? The unique four chamber technology provides a deep clean, long-lasting freshness, fiber care, and a boost of fresh scent.
